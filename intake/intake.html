    Notes

    • Privacy: All conversion runs inside your browser. No files are sent to any server.
    • Scanned PDFs: Image-only PDFs produce little or no output. Text-layer PDFs work well.
    • Password-protected files: Remove password protection before converting.
    • PPTX: Slide text is extracted. Charts and embedded images are not included.
    • EML email: Headers and plain-text body are extracted. HTML-only emails may be partial.
    • Internet required: Conversion libraries load from public CDNs on first use.
