## Assignment Rules

1. Primary owner must match the PRIMARY ROUTE.
2. Supporting field, appraisal, witness, scene, or statement tasks may be assigned separately.
3. If internal staff are within target workload, recommend internal staff first.
4. If internal staff are at workload limit and the task is urgent, recommend approved vendor or alternate queue.
5. If SIU or coverage review is involved, do not recommend a named handler; use supervisor/SIU queue.
6. Named assignments are demo recommendations only. Label all named assignments as demo/test assignments.
